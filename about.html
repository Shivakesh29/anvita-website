About RF and Microwave Technology

RF (Radio Frequency) and Microwave technologies are critical pillars of modern communication and sensing systems. Operating within the electromagnetic spectrum, RF typically covers frequencies from 3 kHz to 300 GHz, while Microwave frequencies generally range from 300 MHz to 300 GHz.

These technologies enable wireless communication, radar, satellite systems, and advanced defense electronics. RF and Microwave components—such as amplifiers, filters, antennas, and transceivers—are at the core of systems used in telecommunications, aerospace, and military applications.

At Anvita RF and Microwave Systems, we specialize in the design and development of high-performance RF and Microwave solutions tailored for demanding environments. Our innovations drive secure communication, precision sensing, and mission-critical performance across industries.
